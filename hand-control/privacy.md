# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 29, 2026**

VGL Studio ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Hand Control mobile application ("App"). This App is provided as a free service.

## Camera and Microphone

Hand Control requires access to your device camera to function. The camera is used to:

- Detect your hand position and gestures so the App can render real-time magic and visual effects
- Show the live camera preview and let you record videos of those effects

The App also requests microphone access, which is used **only** when you choose to record a video with sound.

Your camera and microphone feeds are processed **in real time on your device**. We do not upload, transmit, or store your camera or microphone data on our servers, and we do not use them to identify you.

## On-Device Hand Tracking

Hand and gesture detection is performed entirely on your device using on-device machine learning (Google MediaPipe). No camera images or video frames used for hand tracking ever leave your device through this feature.

## Your Recordings and Content

Videos you record with the App are saved locally on your device (in your device storage or media gallery). You own and control this content. We do not access, collect, or upload your recordings. Any sharing of a recording happens only when you actively choose to share it through another app on your device.

## Information We Collect

We may collect the following information automatically when you use the App:

- **Device Information:** IP address, device type, operating system and version, Advertising ID (GAID)
- **Usage Data:** features used, session duration, and anonymous app performance and diagnostics data

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number unless you voluntarily provide it (for example, when contacting us by email). The App does not collect precise location information.

## How We Use the Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- To provide, operate, and maintain the App's features
- To analyze performance and fix technical issues via Google Analytics for Firebase
- To configure features remotely via Firebase Remote Config
- To display advertisements through Google AdMob

## Advertising

Our App displays advertisements through Google AdMob and its mediation partners, which may include **AppLovin**, **Meta Audience Network**, and **ironSource**. These services may use cookies, device identifiers, or your Advertising ID to serve advertisements, including personalized ads based on your interests. You can opt out of personalized advertising or reset your Advertising ID through your device settings.

For more information on how Google uses your data, please visit [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

- **Google Play Services** — for core functionality
- **Google AdMob** (including mediation partners AppLovin, Meta Audience Network, and ironSource) — for displaying advertisements
- **Google Analytics for Firebase** — for anonymous usage analytics and diagnostics
- **Firebase Remote Config** — for remote feature and configuration management

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing the use of your information.

We retain collected data for as long as you use the App and for a reasonable time thereafter. If you would like us to delete your data, please contact us using the information below.

Our App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.
