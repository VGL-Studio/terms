# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 9, 2026**

VGL Studio ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the BoomTap: 2Player mobile application ("App"). This App is provided as a free service.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

We may collect the following information automatically when you use the App:

- **Device Information:** IP address, device type, Android version, Advertising ID (GAID)
- **Usage Data:** Playtime, game progress, app crashes and crash reports

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number unless you voluntarily provide it.

## How We Use the Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- To provide and maintain game features
- To analyze performance and fix technical issues via Google Analytics and Firebase
- To display relevant advertisements through AdMob

## Advertising

Our App displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may use cookies or device identifiers to serve personalized ads based on your interests. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

For more information on how Google uses your data, please visit [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Data Sharing with Third Parties

We may share data with the following service providers:

- **Google Play Services** — to store game progress and achievements
- **Advertising Partners** — to personalize the advertising experience

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing the use of your information.

## Data Security

We implement standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

## User Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. You can also opt out of all data collection by uninstalling the App.

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at: [vgldream01@gmail.com](mailto:vgldream01@gmail.com)

## Children's Privacy

This application does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13, in compliance with Google Play policies. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the App with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
